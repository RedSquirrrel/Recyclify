How does throwing plastic away affect us?

Throwing plastic away carelessly can affect ocean animals. Some of the plastic might be eaten by fish, and then the fish caught by us humans. We eat the fish and therefore we ate a small amount of plastic. It's a problem to eat plastic because it can cause cancers, immune system suppresion and many more issues.

What can you do?

Recycle regularly and don't ever throw plastic on the ground. If you see anyone else do that then tell them to pick it up and put it in a bin.
